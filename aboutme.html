About Antony Gordon

Follow @tonedefmusic

Imagine the result of technology meeting creativity. Combine that with musical eclecticisms and you could surely say one is fairly diverse. That is only the basics discussed when one refers to the mastermind behind ToneDef Music, Mr. Antony Gordon

Antony Gordon has been working in the Detroit music scene for over 25 years. He first got his start composing and arranging tracks for the Christian hip hop group LiveStock (he was also a member), but has since arranged and co- produced songs for a host of local and national artists. He has gained a reputation for his attention to detail as it relates to learning music, learning the main parts as well as auxiliary parts. This attention to music has afforded him the opportunity to serve as supporting keyboardist as well as main keyboardist for artists.

He has shared the stage with a veritable who's who in the music industry including The Dramatics, Glenn Jones, The Daz Band, Lin Rountree, Althea Rene, Robert Glasper, Mike Phillips, Phil Denny, Julian Vaughn, Stokley Williams (from Mint Condition) and Shaun LaBelle. He has also graced the stages of Detroit's premier venues such as The Fox Theatre, Chene Park, The Music Hall for Performing Arts, and The Charles H. Wright Museum.

Not only is Antony a proficient live musician, he has also lended his creativity and sound design knowledge to several recording projects, his most notable production credit to date is Lin Rountree's cover of "You Rock My World" on his Soul Tree project.
