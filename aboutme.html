Profile for Antony Gordon

Imagine the result of technology meeting creativity. Combine that with musical eclecticisms and you could surely say one is fairly diverse. That is only the basics discussed when one refers to the mastermind behind ToneDef Music, Mr. Antony Gordon

Over the past 20 years, Antony has graced the stage with phenomenal artists such as: Fred Hammond, John P. Kee, The O'Jays, The Whispers, The Dells, The Delfonics, Heatwave, Rose Royce, The Stylistics, Carl Carlton, Jr., Larry Carlton, as well as playing and recording with jazz artists Lin Rountree and Althea Rene. He has co-wrote and co-produced songs for several artists.
